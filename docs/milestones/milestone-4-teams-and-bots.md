# Milestone 4: teams and bots

## Goal

Test teams and bot substitution.

## Build

- team assignment,
- team scores,
- any player can be human or bot,
- random bot,
- greedy bot,
- bot-vs-bot autoplay,
- simple replay log.

## Done when

- 1v1 human vs bot works,
- bot vs bot works,
- 4-player free-for-all with any bot mix works,
- 2v2 team scoring works,
- move log records bot and human moves.
